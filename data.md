At Techzgreen, we transform plastic waste—especially Multi-Layer Plastic (MLP)—into durable and valuable products through innovative recycling and upcycling technologies. Our mission is to help industries, institutions, and communities implement effective waste management systems while converting non-valuable plastic waste into high-quality, long-lasting materials. By doing this, we contribute to closing the circular loop of plastic and promoting a sustainable future.

We manufacture a wide range of eco-friendly products such as school benches, tables, furniture, outdoor benches, TV units, roofing sheets, flooring panels, ceiling boards, partitions, and custom items like momentos. These products are designed to be waterproof, pest-proof, fungus-resistant, high-density, and long-lasting, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Beyond manufacturing, we also provide consultation and technical guidance to companies and organizations on how to manage plastic waste more effectively and achieve sustainable waste management goals. Through our solutions, waste becomes a resource—creating environmental impact, sustainable advertising opportunities, and a cleaner planet for future generations.

TechzGreen Eco Product's high-performance Z pallets are designed for exceptional strength, durability and load-bearing capacity, making them ideal for the toughest industrial applications with Z boards, we manufacture heavy-duty pallets that with stand extreme conditions, ensuring unmatched reliability and longevity in material handling.

Z Momento – Turning Plastic Waste into Purpose

Z Momento is a premium, eco-friendly momento and trophy handcrafted from recycled plastic and MLP (Multi-Layer Plastic) waste. Designed by Techzgreen, a sustainable consulting startup, this product transforms one of today’s most challenging waste streams into a symbol of responsibility, innovation, and environmental commitment.

Single-use plastic is deeply embedded in our daily lives, yet its disposal remains a global challenge. At Techzgreen, we address this problem at its root by professionally recycling and upcycling plastic waste into beautiful, durable, and meaningful momentos.

Each Z Momento is thoughtfully designed to look elegant and modern while carrying a powerful message of sustainability. Whether given as an award, corporate gift, or recognition trophy, it represents a conscious step toward a cleaner future.
